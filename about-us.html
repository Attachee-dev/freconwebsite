Who we are

Today the challenge is not to conquer the moon but the quality of life on earth. Our world desperately needs the implementation of clean technologies to reduce energy consumption and the waste of natural resources. Renewable energy is way more than just a solution for climate change, it puts the environment, humanity, and economic security first – that’s sustainability: a sign of progress. The above stated notion drove to the formation of Frecon Solar, the journey started on the dawn of 2012 as the company penetrated the market as an informal trader and was later on registered in 2016. Over the past few years, the company has undertake many projects in solar water pumping systems, home borehole installations and consultancy. Although started with a modest amount of capital but today Frecon Solar is growing fast in every aspect to try and meet the demand of the exponentially growing solar industry. We are supported by a strong management team comprising people with relevant technical experience. We strive to provide excellent renewable energy solutions to satisfy the challenging need of our customers. Our customers are an evidence for our achievement.
